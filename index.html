LUKE 18 : 1 - 8

1 One day Jesus told his disciples a story to show that they should always pray and never give up. 2 "There was a judge in a certain city," he said, "who neither feared God nor cared about people. 3 A widow of that city came to him repeatedly, saying, 'Give me justice in this dispute with my enemy. 4 The judge ignored her for a while, but finaly he said to himself, 'I don't fear God or care about people, 5 but this woman is driving me crazy. i'm going to see that she gets justice, because she is wearing me out with her constant requests!" 6 Then the Lord said, "Learn a lesson from this unjust judge. 7 Even he rendered a just decision in the end. So don't you think God will surely give to his chosen people who cry out to his chosen people who cry out to him day and night? Will he keep putting the off?

About GWPFI

God Winners Prayer Force Int Ministry was instablished in 13 March 2013. The founder of the church is Apostle TM Sibandze. The name God Winners Prayer Force Int Ministry describes the the working power of our Lord Jesus Christ who died for our sins and raised to life by The power of our God. It also describe the power of prayer as one to defeat the devel. We invite anyone to come fellowship with every sunday 10:00 - 13:00 and Wednesday 18:00 - 19:00 Salvation is the Goal.
